MARVEL PRONE

AKA DAVE

We are an indie rock band hailing from the New Hampshire seacoast. The band blends psychedelic, alternative, and garage rock. Their albums include Moonlighting (2016), She Hits Me (2020), Tres Hombres (2021), and 11:22 (2022), the last of which was recorded with the acclaimed sound engineer Steve Albini. Marvel Prone puts on raw, electrifying performances that take listeners on a sonic journey through heaven and hell, combining elements of romance and heartbreak in Rainor Tsunami's ambitious songwriting style. They've played notable venues in NH and Boston such as The Stone Church, The Press Room, The 3S Art Space, and The Middle East.
